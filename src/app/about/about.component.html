The Beginning

With its inception in April 2011, ALM had a burning ambition to succeed in the Qatari market with the available resources then. We knew very well that the odds were heavily stacked against us. However, our optimistic approach and attitude helped us in leading the competition in terms of the products, prices and professional services.

With over 7 years of experience in the industry, our services have always been exceptional as compared to our competitors in terms of bearing professional staff, on-time delivery of projects, and trained and skilled on-ground personnel to efficiently handle client requirements.

ALM always had an attitude of perseverance along with substantial market confidence in Qatar (the modern era's "land of opportunities"). We were convinced that we could strengthen our position in the market through our value proposition and credibility established with our clients.
