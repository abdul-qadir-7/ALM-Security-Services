ALM SECURITY MANNED SERVICES

Helping businesses succeed is what we do. We provide security services to our clients so that they can focus on their core business. These services comprise:

Static Guarding

Static Guarding is security manpower in a set place. This may involve manned services on access points, the guarding of equipment or valuables as part of loss prevention, crowd control, event security, scene securing, or personal protection, essentially anything requiring a person being onsite. We provide checks at regular intervals at night across location providing our clients with peace of mind and ensuring operational continuity.

Facility Management

HOSPITALITY SERVICES such as Office Boys, Housekeeping staff, Drivers staff, Valet Parking Services, Receptionist, Corporate services / Back-office support, Lifeguard Services.

MAINTENANCE SERVICES such as Electrical Maintenance, A/C maintenance, Plumbing Maintenance, BMS Operator, Painters, Carpentry Services.

GARDENING SERVICES

Vip Security

VIP security provides highly-specialized professional service that is dedicated to ensuring the personal security of celebrities, athletes, artists, CEO’s, and other individuals.
Through us, you have instant access to carefully trained and pre-screened officers to transport and protect business professionals or celebrities en route to important destinations, like events, conferences, or performances.

Cash-In-Transit

Cash-in-transit (CIT) is the secure, physical transportation of money, including banknotes, coins and items of value from one place to another.  Our cash collection service uses trained personnel and a fleet of armoured money transport trucks to pick up cash at your chosen location. From the moment your cash is collected and transported it's our responsibility, so you have peace of mind.
The locations include cash centers, bank branches, ATM points and large retailers 

Electronic Security and Surveillance

SECURITY MANAGEMENT SYSTEMS (SMS) offers surveillance systems, perimeter and interior intrusion alarm systems and hosted solutions.
ACCESS CONTROL SYSTEMS offers serial controllers, intelligent readers and network-enabled controllers.
INTRUSION DETECTION SYSTEMS offers Intrusion Detection solutions that monitor and analyse user and system activities, analyse system configurations and vulnerabilities and tracks user policy violations.

Technical Services

Technical services include security management systems, access control systems and intrusion detection systems with intruder alarms, fire protection equipment, access control, closed circuit television, perimeter site protection, and alarms monitoring systems, integrated security systems, physical security, electronic time recording and service, maintenance and repair services. We provide hardware and software options to make it easy and economical to expand a system that needs to keep up with growing business needs.

Total plant protection

FIRE ALARM & FIRE SUPPRESSION SYSTEMS.
Whether it is residential or commercial, hotel, hospital, or oil & gas projects, we offer complete design and implementation for all fire alarm systems while adhering the civil defense and international standards.
For complete plant protection, we provide entry management, vehicle scanning, X-Ray and Intercom systems to name a few.
With our whole gamut of network-based solutions, our sound reinforcement systems can grace the smallest of restaurants to the largest of factories

Special Event or Concert

Experience of dealing with public gatherings of all sizes is another of our specialist areas. We can compile a plan suitable for the event that will take into consideration everything from the needs of your guests through to the safety of your performers, while maintaining a discreet yet effective presence.

Retail Security

Either uniformed store guards or plain-clothes store detectives play a major role in our retail security services. Activities are not confined to preventing or apprehending shoplifters: dishonesty and wastage, Test purchasing and inventory controls are among the other areas monitored and covered by our services. A variety of security products, such as loop alarms, security / safety mirrors, electronic article surveillance, and closed-circuit television are also available for retail outlets.
